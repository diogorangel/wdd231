NP Membership Benefits

This membership is exclusively for non-profit organizations and comes with no fee.

  • Access to basic chamber events
  • Inclusion in the online business directory
  • Receive chamber newsletter

This is a great option for organizations looking to get involved with the community without a financial commitment.

×

Bronze Membership Benefits

The entry-level paid membership for small businesses.

  • All NP Membership benefits
  • Discounted access to selected training workshops
  • Priority registration for general chamber events
  • Basic listing in the printed business directory
  • Annual Fee: $100
×

Silver Membership Benefits

An intermediate membership offering more exposure and resources.

  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • Exclusive invitations to members-only networking events
  • 25% discount on all chamber training and workshops
  • Enhanced listing in online and printed directories
  • Opportunity to host one chamber event per year (venue fees apply)

Annual Fee: $250

×

Gold Membership Benefits

Our premium membership, ideal for established businesses seeking maximum impact.

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Prominent spotlight position on the homepage for one month per year
  • Free access to all chamber training and workshops
  • Guaranteed speaking slots at quarterly chamber forums
  • Dedicated account manager for personalized support
  • Priority access to all special events and sponsorship opportunities

Annual Fee: $500
